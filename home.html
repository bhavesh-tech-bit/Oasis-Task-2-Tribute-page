Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

Why I Admire Him

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was not just a scientist but a visionary leader. He played a major role in India's space and missile development programs. His dedication to the nation inspires millions of people.

What I admire most about him is his simplicity and humility. Even after becoming the President of India, he remained grounded and connected with students and young minds.

He believed that dreams are not what you see in sleep, but dreams are those that do not let you sleep. His words continue to motivate me to work hard and never give up.
